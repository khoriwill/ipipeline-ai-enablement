# AI Enablement Success Metrics

**The rule: if you can't measure it, you can't defend it in the next budget cycle.**

This document defines how AI enablement success is measured — from individual agent performance to program-level ROI.

---

## The Three Layers of Measurement

### Layer 1 — Activity Metrics (Are people using it?)
### Layer 2 — Efficiency Metrics (Is it saving time?)
### Layer 3 — Business Impact Metrics (Is it moving the needle?)

Most AI programs only measure Layer 1. That's why they lose budget. This framework measures all three.

---

## Layer 1: Activity Metrics

Tracked automatically via Copilot Studio analytics dashboard.

| Metric | Description | Target (90 days) |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly active users | Unique users interacting with agents per week | >80% of target team |
| Sessions per user per week | How often individuals return | >3x per week |
| Topics triggered | Which conversation flows are being used | All 4 core topics active |
| Escalation to human rate | % of queries the agent couldn't resolve | <20% |
| User satisfaction (thumbs up/down) | In-agent feedback | >80% positive |

**What low activity tells you:** Adoption problem — training, awareness, or the agent isn't where the work happens.

---

## Layer 2: Efficiency Metrics

Tracked via before/after measurement and periodic rep surveys.

| Metric | Measurement Method | Baseline | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Time to find SOP | Timed observation or survey | 8–12 min | <2 min |
| Ticket summarization time | Survey + ticket timestamps | 15–20 min | <3 min |
| Escalation decision time | Survey | 10–15 min | <5 min |
| Hours saved per rep per week | Weekly survey (2 questions) | 0 | 3–5 hrs |
| Repetitive questions to team lead | Team lead log | TBD baseline | -40% |

**How to capture baseline:** Before launch, ask 5–10 PS reps: "How long does it take you to [task]?" That's your baseline. Run the same survey at 30, 60, and 90 days.

---

## Layer 3: Business Impact Metrics

Tracked in coordination with PS leadership and business analytics.

| Metric | Why It Matters | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| PS implementation cycle time | Faster implementations = faster revenue recognition | Project management system |
| Client time-to-value | How quickly clients go live after contract | CRM / PS tracking |
| PS rep capacity (implementations per quarter) | If reps save 4 hrs/week, do they take on more work? | Headcount vs. delivery volume |
| Support ticket resolution time | Faster triage = better client experience | ServiceNow |
| Escalation rate trends | Fewer unnecessary escalations = senior staff freed up | ServiceNow |

## ROI Calculation Framework

**Conservative estimate for a 10-person PS team:**

```
Hours saved per rep per week:     4 hours
Team size:                        10 reps
Total hours saved per week:       40 hours
Weeks per quarter:                13
Total hours saved per quarter:    520 hours

Average fully-loaded hourly cost: $75/hour (conservative)
Quarterly value generated:        $39,000
Annual value generated:           $156,000
```

**This is the number you bring to the next budget conversation.**

Additional value not captured in hours:
- Reduced errors from stale documentation
- Consistent client communication during escalations
- Faster onboarding for new PS reps (agent as training tool)
- Scalability — same team handles more implementations

## Reporting Cadence

### 30-Day Report (to PS leadership)
- Active user count and adoption rate
- Top 3 most-used topics
- Early efficiency survey results
- Issues encountered and resolved
- Recommendation: continue, adjust, or pause

### 60-Day Report (to department heads)
- Full efficiency metrics vs. baseline
- ROI estimate with actuals
- User feedback themes
- Expansion recommendation (ready for Customer Support pilot?)

### 90-Day Report (to executive leadership)
- Full program ROI
- Business impact metrics
- Governance review summary
- Phase 2 roadmap recommendation
- Budget ask for next phase

## Dashboard

All metrics will be visible in a Microsoft Power BI dashboard connected to:
- Copilot Studio analytics (activity metrics)
- ServiceNow (ticket metrics)
- Quarterly survey results (efficiency metrics)

The dashboard is accessible to AI Enablement Lead, PS leadership, and executive sponsors.
